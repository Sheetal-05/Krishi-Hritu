About

Aiming to help the agricultural community, KrishiHritu agricultural services is an intiative to update and warn the farmers about the weather in the coming days can help them to stay alert. Messages will be sent out once a week informing the farmers about the weather in upcoming days.

Resources

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund

A pan India Central Sector Scheme providing debt financing facility for investment in Agri infrastructure.

100+ videos on Youtube

100+ videos are available on YouTube educating farmers about various schemes and facilities provided by the government.

National Mission For Sustainable Agriculture (NMSA)

The scheme intends to make the farmer sustainable and agriculture a productive and lucrative business. Farmers can use new and modern techniques in farming.

Krishi UDAN scheme

The Krishi UDAN 2.0 will be implemented at 53 airports across the country mainly focusing on Northeast and tribal regions and is likely to benefit farmer, freight forwarders and airlines.

PM Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana

Scheme to provide social security to Small and Marginal Farmers in their old age.

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY)

Farmers are encouraged to form clusters or groups. Each cluster or group has 50 farmers willing to take up organic farming. The aim is to form 10,000 clusters over the coming years.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

It is a crop insurance scheme to provide the farmers a stable source of income, where multiple stakeholders are integrated on a single platform.

Gramin Bhandaran Yojana

Under this scheme, the government aims to provide storage facilities to the farmer in rural areas.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

This scheme was launched in 1998 to provide credit facilities to farmers. The scheme intends to provide enough credit to farmers for their agriculture expenses.
